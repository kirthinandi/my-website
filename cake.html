Strawberry Tres Leches Cake

Ingredients
-Super Moist Vanilla Cake Mix (1 box)
-Water (1 cup)
-Vegetable Oil (1 cup)
-Eggs (3)
-Mexican Vanilla Extract (2 tbsp: 1 in cake mix, 1 in whipped cream)
-Half and Half (2 cups)
-Condensed Milk (14 ounces)
-Evaporated Milk (12 ounces)
-Heavy Cream (2 cups)
-Sugar (3 tbsp)
-Sliced Strawberries (1 cup)
Directions
  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Grease bottom of pan with oil or butter.
  2. Mix cake mix, water, oil, Mexican vanilla, and eggs in a large bowl with mixer or whisk. Pour the mixture into the greased pan.
  3. Bake for approximately 25-30 minutes while checking on the cake every 10-15 minutes.
  4. After checking that the cake has been fully cooked, wait for it to cool down. This could take 20-45 minutes.
  5. While the cake is cooling down, mix the half and half, evaporated milk, and condensed milk in another large bowl using a whisk.
  6. Set the milk mixture to the side and cut a thin layer off the top of the cake.
  7. Poke the whole cake with a fork so that the milk can be absorbed into the cake easily.
  8. Next, pour the milk mixture onto the cake evenly for equal distribution.
  9. Once you are done pouring the milk, cover the cake with foil or plastic wrap and place the cake in the fridge for atleast 3 hours or overnight for the best result.
  10. Pour the heavy cream, sugar, and Mexican vanilla into a blender and blend until it reaches the desired texture.
  11. Top the cake with the whipped cream and sliced strawberries to your liking. Your cake is now ready to be enjoyed!
