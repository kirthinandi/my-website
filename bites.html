Lemon Bites

Ingredients
-Flour (2 cups)
-Sugar (1/2 cup)
-Salt (1/4 teaspoon)
-Salted Butter (2 sticks, 1 cup; cut into small cubes)
-Sugar (1.5 cup; for filling)
-Flour (1/4 cup; for filling)
-Large Eggs (4; for filling)
-Medium-sized lemons (4; zest and juice)
-Powdered Sugar (sifting; for decoration)
Directions
  1. For the crust: Preheat the oven to 350Â°F. Grease a 9-by-13-inch pan with butter. If you want a thicker crust, you can use a 9x9 pan instead.
  2. Stir together the flour, sugar, and salt. Add the butter to the bowl and use a pastry cutter to cut it all together until the mixture resembles fine crumbs.
  3. Press into the prepared pan and bake until golden around the edges for about 20 minutes.
  4. For the filling: Stir together the sugar and flour. Crack in the eggs and whisk to combine.
  5. Add the lemon zest and juice and mix until combined. Pour over the crust and bake about 20 minutes.
  6. Allow to cool in the fridge for a minimum of 2 hours, then sift powdered sugar over the top before cutting into squares. Now your lemon bites are ready to be the talk of the next party!
  7. Store leftover lemon bars in the fridge.
